Meditate Like a Superhero!

It's valuable, It's fun, and It's free!!

We have designed this program for children and adults to learn a form of meditation that develops inner strength, leadership skills and a realization of their true potential all of which is carried out in a free and fun-filled environment.






Thankyou all for attending the event, it was fun having you all !!

About

The Program

The program includes meditation practice sessions, arts & crafts and beautifully illustrated children’s stories that focus on kindness and respect. The participants will be served a free delicious vegetarian meal. Also, each participant is welcome to an array of healthy snacks that will delight children and parents alike.

Feedback

Parent participants have often applauded how the program emphasizes meditation and encourages a deeper reflection of self for them and bonding with their children. As a result of this program you will achieve the following life-long skills:

  • Development of inner strength through meditation
  • Enhancement of your leadership skills
  • Realization of your true potential in a fun-filled and relaxed environment
